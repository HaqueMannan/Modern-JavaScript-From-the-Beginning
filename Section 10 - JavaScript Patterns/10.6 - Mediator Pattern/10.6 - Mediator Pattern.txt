Section 10.6. Mediator Pattern:

--------------------------------------
Mediator Pattern
--------------------------------------
- Another behavioural pattern like the Observer Pattern. 
- The logic behind this pattern is to have a mediator and colleagues.
- Mediators is a interfaces for communicating with colleagues.
- Colleagues are mediated objects.
- The best example of a Mediator Pattern is a chatroom.

- Please refer to the js_sandbox_10.6 folder for example of Mediator Pattern.
